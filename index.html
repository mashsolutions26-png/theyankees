ABOUT US

The story behind Yankee's Restaurant & Bar

Bringing American Street Food to Australia

Yankee's Restaurant & Bar brings the vibrant essence of American street food culture to Australia with three prime locations in Melbourne, Surfers Paradise, and Hope Island.

Known for its juicy, meaty burgers, hearty hot dogs, refreshing drinks, and lively atmosphere, Yankee's is the perfect destination for indulgent flavors and good times.
